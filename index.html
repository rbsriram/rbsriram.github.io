Magic & Football

One of my favorite stories about Arsenal Football Club is the legend of Herbert Chapman, the manager who revolutionized the game in the 1930s. Chapman introduced the WM formation and was a pioneer in using floodlights for evening matches. His innovative thinking laid the foundation for Arsenal's success and has influenced football strategies for decades.
